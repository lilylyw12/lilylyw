Lily's Original Digital Media Project

Project 1:

I chose the media major course LSC 432 in the fall semester of the 2023 academic year. Our group project for this class was "Billie's Soap." This brand produces natural bath, cleaning and personal products. Our team's project content is to help them expand their markets, create better turnover, and help them better operate online social media. They use organic ingredients at affordable prices, giving their products a solid foundation. Our team helped them redesign the layout of their website and the operation of their Instagram and Twitter official accounts. We also try to let them use live broadcasts to sell goods online in order to obtain higher sales.
